# Roadmap

The current publication scope is complete when installation, tests,
documentation, and the live dashboard remain reproducible from a clean clone.
The items below are optional future ideas, not publication requirements:

- support richer delay or state-space models;
- add path-specific time constants or delays if the predictor structure is
  deliberately expanded;
- explore scaled per-CV priorities, per-MV move penalties, multiple objectives,
  and soft constraints;
- investigate confidence intervals or more advanced excitation diagnostics.

Major process-model or controller changes should be developed separately from
publication and maintenance work.
